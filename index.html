About US

IEEE BPIT is a technical community of Bhagwan Parshuram Institute of Technology that imparts the technical knowledge amongst students for their forthcoming life in order to give them a new professional outlook and encourages them to participate in various technical competitions held . We are a community of active engineering students to learn new skills and mentors who help them to new impact and difference in this competive world through their experience.

Mission

To help Engineering Students to develop technical skills and conduct Industry-Institute for developing technical competency and imbibing corporate culture among themselves . To promote collaborative learning as well as create a communiy where can enhance their problem-solving ability and much more

Vision

IEEE BPIT aims to make the students aware about the recent advances in technology and recognizes the contribution of technology and technical professionals in the global world.
